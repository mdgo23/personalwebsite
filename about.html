About Marelyn Gonzalez

My first encounter with programming that sparked a curiosity of technology was during my junior year of high school while interning at the RYSE Center in Richmond, California. I worked with my teammate to present our research on a website. Given that we had no coding knowledge, we designed our website and tackled issues I would come to find out were related to user experience and User interface design. During my senior year, I was mentored by someone at Figma and deepened my learning of design principles. I hope to continue my learning journey as I work on new projects!

My favorite technologies include:

  • HTML
  • CSS
  • Figma
